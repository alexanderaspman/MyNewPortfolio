Hjälpenhemlös

Hjälpenhemlös.se is an initiative of the company Jobb & Bostad where together they can offer the homeless a more dignified standard. It is a Malmö project that with the help of local business and the private sector collects different types of resources which are then shared with the homeless & needy around Malmö by bicycle.

I helped Hjälpenhemlös.se to create their logotype in vector for their merchandise and website. I was also admin for their Facebook group and helped with the UX of their new website.

Used techniques: Illustrator, Wordpress
